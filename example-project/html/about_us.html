Our Difference

We are an educational institution dedicated to promoting Bahá’í principles and teachings for the common good.

We seek to provide innovative and transformative learning experiences for college students and others who want to make the world more compassionate, just and inclusive.

Courses for University Credit

Our courses are designed to meet online learning standards and best practices, for university credit and continuing education. We’re always open to new university and college partnership opportunities.

Community Learning Courses

We offer courses on the Bahá’í Approach to Social Transformation, Bahá’í History and Texts, Marriage and Family, Religion, Philosophy and Theology. We also offer courses in Arts and Literature and the Development of the Bahá’i Community.

Our Diverse & Distinguished Faculty

Over 75 faculty serve our students. They reside on four continents. Many of them have doctoral degrees and several are distinguished authors. We are always seeking diverse individuals to add to our faculty and enrich our learning community.

Our Governance

“We cherish the hope that... the power of perception [may be] enhanced, [and] that the people may discover the purpose for which they have been called into being.”

Bahá’u’llah

We are an educational agency of the National Spiritual Assembly of the Bahá’ís of the United States, established in 1995. Our dedicated staff manage our day-to-day operations. An academic advisory board of distinguished scholars and instructors help us achieve our educational vision.

The Bahá’í Way

​We believe it’s time for humanity to come together as one family, to create a world where everyone can thrive.
